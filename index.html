Fashion Retail Has Unique Loss Prevention Challenges.

Apparel stores are a high-noise environment: constant product handling, fitting rooms and more. Generic retail AI often struggles to stay practical for store staff.

High interaction “noise”

Customers touch, compare, carry, and try on items — normal behavior looks suspicious in many generic systems.

Fitting rooms & blind spots

Visibility drops precisely where risk increases. Fashion needs a setup that accounts for real store layout.

Staff-first response

Many stores rely on sales associates — alerts must be actionable and low on false alarms.

Context-aware AI, built for fashion.

AegisCV is building a next-generation loss prevention system designed specifically for fashion retail. The current system focuses on detecting high-risk concealment events in real time.

Fashion-first focus

Built around apparel-store scenarios, not grocery-style assumptions.

Pilot-driven development

We refine detection and reduce false alerts using real store conditions and feedback.

Roadmap to deeper context

Expanding toward broader context-aware risk evaluation tailored to fashion environments.
